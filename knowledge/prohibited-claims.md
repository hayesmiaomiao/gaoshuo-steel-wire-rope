# Prohibited Claims

The website and future Codex sessions must not fabricate:

- ISO certification
- CE certification
- API certification
- Customer count
- Export countries
- Production years
- Monthly capacity
- Equipment quantity
- Test reports
- Customer logos
- Engineering cases
- Lead time
- MOQ
- Breaking load
- Material grade
- Standards compliance
- Testing capability
- Product safety rating or safety-critical suitability
- Source website contact details
- Source website company identity
- Source website logos, maps, QR codes, social links or copyright text
