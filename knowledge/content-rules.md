# Content Rules

- Website content must be professional English.
- Content must focus on procurement, product selection, customization and verified capability.
- Draft content remains `draft` and `noindex`.
- Do not create thin city pages, keyword-stuffed pages, or duplicated product variants.
- Use TODO markers in knowledge files, not in polished front-end copy unless needed for maintainers.
- Product and service content from user-approved source websites may be reused after brand cleanup, contact cleanup, deduplication, SEO rewriting, and validation. Source domains are stored only in `research/`.
- Source domains may be stored in internal research files and migration audit fields, but must not appear as front-end marketing copy.
