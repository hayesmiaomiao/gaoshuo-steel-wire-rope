# Product Rules

## Confirmed Scope

- Steel Wire Rope
- Stainless Steel Wire Rope
- Galvanized Steel Wire Rope
- Coated Steel Wire Rope
- 7x7 Wire Rope
- 7x19 Wire Rope
- 6x19 Wire Rope
- Custom Wire Rope Assemblies
- Wire Rope Fittings
- Steel Wire Rope from approximately 0.1 mm to 24 mm as a general business range

## Rules

- Product-specific diameter ranges must come from product data.
- Do not infer breaking load, material grade, standards, MOQ, lead time, or certifications.
- New product records start as `draft`.
